III.   SERVICE AND BENEFITS   
Please describe the nature of the service that your organization provides to York County. 
Describe/explain how York County government is, or will be, benefited by the service that your 
organization provides, or proposes to provide, to County residents. Identify the units of service provided 
to York County during the most recently concluded fiscal year.   
 
“Unit of Service” should be defined as it relates to the organization (# of clients, # of visits, # of hours, # 
of volunteers, # of jobs performed, etc).  Example: 10 York County residents served twice daily, five 
days a week, over the course of the year: 10 residents x 2 services per day x 5 days per week x 52 
weeks per year = 5,200 units.
