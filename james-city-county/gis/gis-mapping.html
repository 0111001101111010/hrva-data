GIS/Mapping Section - James City County

GIS Layers

The following is a list GIS layers available from the Mapping/GIS Section. Most layers can be downloaded here for free in ESRI Shapefile format. The files have been compressed (.zip) for faster download. A link has been provided if the data is from an external resource. Data is current as of Jan. 1, 2013. Metadata is available: GIS Metadata PDF

If software is needed to view these layers, an application is available for free download: ESRI ArcExplorer

James City County makes every reasonable effort to ensure the accuracy of the data set. Within the urban areas of the County, we hope to have a plus or minus 5-foot accuracy. Out in the rural areas, where sometimes we don't have any recently recorded plats, the accuracy could go higher to almost a plus or minus 20 feet.
