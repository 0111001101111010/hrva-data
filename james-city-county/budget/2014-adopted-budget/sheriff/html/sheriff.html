BUDGET COMMENTS 

The City of Williamsburg pays for 17.26 percent of the local cost of this office in FY2014.  The Sheriff 
also generates other court-related fees to help reduce overall local funding.  This budget includes funding 
for three vehicle replacements.  Local Aid to the State Government has been eliminated.
