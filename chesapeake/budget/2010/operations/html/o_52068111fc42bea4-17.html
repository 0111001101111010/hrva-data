PUBLIC WORKS - DRAINAGE

100-112040-41310

DESCRIPTION

The Drainage division provides maintenance and drainage improvement activities throughout the 
City.

GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

Goal:  Maintain and improve the condition of existing pipe systems within the City’s right-of-
ways.

Objectives:
▪  Rehabilitate 400 linear feet of pipe to extend the life 20 years.
▪  Clean and/or repair 1500 catch basins to minimize sediment infiltration.

Goal:  Excavate new and existing drainage systems in conjunction with the Master Drainage Plan.

Objectives:
▪  Clean 230,000 linear feet of ditch as warranted.
▪  Rehabilitate 100,000 linear feet of ditch as warranted.

SERVICE LEVEL ANALYSIS

Funding for City Internal Service Fund charges have been reduced.
