REVENUE BUDGET                                                                                            DESCRIPTION OF FUNDS

     Established during the FY04 budgeting process in order to account for revenues and expenditures related to operation 
of the Emergency Operations Center (EOC).  The revenues in this Fund are restricted by the State to be used exclusively 
for EOC operations. These activities were previously budgeted under the General Fund.

Chesapeake Conference Center Fund - 206

Emergency Communications Fund - 207

     Established to account for revenues and expenditures related to the operation of the Chesapeake Conference Center 
which opened during FY 1998 and tourism promotion activities benefiting the local hospitality industry.  The revenues 
come from Restaurant Food Tax (0.5% of 5.5% local tax) and Hotel Room Taxes (1% of 8% local tax, plus $1/room-
night) along with Charges for Services.  Charges for Services include Food Revenue, Beverage Revenue, Sale of Services, 
Rent of Conference Center, Admission Fees, and Service Fees.
