REVENUE BUDGET                                                                                            DESCRIPTION OF FUNDS

Mosquito Control Commission Fund - 800
     Established to account for revenues and expenditure related to the operations of the Mosquito Control Commission of 
the City of Chesapeake.  The primary source of revenue for the Mosquito Control Commission includes General Property 
Taxes on real estate ($0.01/$100) and personal property ($0.08/$100).  The Commission also receives some Interest 
Income on their unspent cash balances. Effective FY-04, five separate district commissions are consolidated into a single 
fund. Effective FY-09 the real estate rate was reduced from $0.02/$100 to $0.01/$100.
