Transfers from/to Other Funds
     A large portion General Fund revenues is transferred to other funds principally to fund school operations, school and 
city-wide debt service, Social Services, Community Services, Interagency Consortium, and capital construction projects. 
The General Fund also receives transfers from other funds of interest earned on cash balances.  The transfer to the school 
operating budget represents by far the largest of the transfers.
