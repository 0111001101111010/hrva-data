Property Tax Penalties and Interest
      The penalty for late payment of property taxes is 10% of the amount due.  The interest on delinquent taxes and 
penalties accrue at a rate of 10% per annum.  In cases of property on which delinquent taxes are not paid within three 
years, the City records a lien on the property effective for twenty years and the property is eligible to be sold through 
judicial proceedings.  There is no limit at the present time on the property tax rates that may be established by the City.
