REVENUE BUDGET                                                                                            DESCRIPTION OF FUNDS

     Established to account for revenues and expenditures related to the operation of the City's public school system.  The 
revenues in these funds come primarily from State agencies for operations and Federal agencies for grants.  The major 
revenue sources are Basic Aid, Sales Tax Revenue, and Special Education funding from the State. The School Funds also 
receive revenues from Grants such as National School Lunch and Breakfast Programs, and Title I and Section 611 Federal 
Grants, as well as from Charges for Services such as sale of meals and tuition for summer school, adult education, and 
driver education. About 41% of school expenses are funded by a transfer from the General Fund (shown in the figure 
above concerning transfers) and not included in this graph.
