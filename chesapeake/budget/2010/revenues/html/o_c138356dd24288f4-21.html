of this revenue source is not restricted as to use (non-categorical), while the rest is a partial reimbursement of local 
expenditures for road maintenance, constitutional officers, and similar activities. For the purpose of the analysis presented 
in these graphs, PPTRA is included with personal property taxes rather than non-categorical aid.
