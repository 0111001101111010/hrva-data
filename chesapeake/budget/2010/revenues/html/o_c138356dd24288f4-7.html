Development
    Real Estate taxes make up the largest single component of the General Fund. Growth in assessed values, therefore 
the tax base, is driven by the market for existing structures, expansions and improvements, and by new construction. In 
addition, construction activity generates employment and subsidiary economic activity, from building supplies to 
restaurant meals. Changes in construction activity can, among other effects, affect taxes generated by retail activity, 
restaurant sales, and business licenses.
